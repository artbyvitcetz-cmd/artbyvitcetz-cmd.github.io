Art By Vitcetz – Imperfectly Perfect –

Some stories are told in words.
Mine are told in colours.

Welcome to my world of art that refuses to sit quietly in the corner, where colours have loud opinions and canvases don’t believe in small talk. This is my playground, a place where I try to colour myself beautifully - Perfectly Imperfect.

Here you'll find the things I make
- definitely not your grandma's landscape painting.
Just colours and shapes, with the occasional hidden naked lady, because life is too short for polite brushstrokes.

You’re welcome 😉

My style? A mash-up of feelings, colours, and a sprinkle of the occasional existential crisis
— you know, the fun kind you get somewhere between finishing a painting and realizing you’re out of snacks.
If you want labels: call it “abstract” or “modern,” or whatever sounds clever at dinner parties.

Personally, I just think of it as paintings without permission slips, and without the pressure of making everything make sense.
Hidden in the chaos you might find shapes, faces, or maybe even the suggestion of something familiar.

Feel free to play a little game of Spot the Naked Lady in my paintings.
